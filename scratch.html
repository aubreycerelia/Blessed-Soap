Cold Press Soap Making

Welcome to my soap-making page! I love creating natural cold press soaps that are good for the skin and fun to make.

Watch the Process

Steps to Make Cold Press Soap

  1. Gather your materials:
    • Oils (olive, coconut, etc.)
    • Lye
    • Essential oils
    • Soap mold
  2. Measure and mix ingredients
  3. Pour into mold and let set
  4. Cut and cure the soap bars
